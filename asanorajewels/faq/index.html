Ordering

Can I inquire about a specific product reference?

Yes. Each catalog card includes a reference code so the concierge can respond quickly with the most relevant styling and availability guidance.

Materials

What kind of finish should I expect from the pieces?

The brand direction centers on polished gold surfaces, smooth lines, and refined contrast details such as enamel or ivory-toned accents depending on the piece.

Materials

Which pieces feel best for daily wear?

Sculpted Chains and selected bracelets are the most versatile for daily styling, while statement pieces and special sets are better suited to occasions and elevated looks.

Care

How should I care for the jewelry between wears?

Store pieces individually when possible, keep them away from moisture and fragrance, and use a soft cloth after wear to maintain a polished finish.

Care

Should statement and enamel pieces be handled differently?

Yes. Statement silhouettes and enamel details benefit from more mindful storage so decorative surfaces remain protected from pressure or friction.

Gifting

Can the concierge help me choose a gift for a specific occasion?

Absolutely. The concierge is designed for anniversaries, birthdays, bridal events, and milestone gifting where the emotional tone matters as much as the product itself.

Gifting

Which collection is usually strongest for gifting?

Celebration Sets and Soft Icons are especially strong for gifts, while Sculpted Chains work beautifully for confident self-gifting and milestone purchases.

Shipping

How quickly should I expect a response after reaching out?

The preview is positioned around a 24 to 48 hour response rhythm for detailed concierge conversations, depending on the nature of the request.

Shipping

Can I mention a deadline for an event or gift?

Yes. Mention the occasion date when you contact the concierge so the recommended edit can account for urgency and presentation needs.
