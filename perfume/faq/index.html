Product

Which fragrance should I start with?

Onyx Essence is the most versatile starting point. If you want more warmth, choose Midnight Saffron. For a cleaner profile, Lunar Vetiver is the most restrained.

Product

Do the fragrances lean masculine or feminine?

The house is intentionally unisex. The compositions are built around mood, texture, and projection rather than gendered marketing.

Product

What sizes are available?

The range is designed around a standard bottle size and a smaller format for travel, testing, or gifting.

Order

How do I place an order?

Use the order page to review the process, then email the bottle name, size, and delivery details to the concierge address.

Order

Can I request a discovery set first?

Yes. The discovery set is the cleanest way to compare the fragrance families before committing to a full bottle.

Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Shipping timing is confirmed in the final order reply so the delivery window matches the destination and packaging request.

Delivery

Do you ship internationally?

International delivery can be discussed in the order request. Share the destination and the concierge will confirm the available options.

Gifting

Can I include a gift note?

Yes. Add the note in the order request and the bottle can be prepared as a gift-ready package.

Care

How should I store the bottle?

Keep the bottle in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. That preserves the structure of the fragrance and the finish of the presentation box.

Care

Will the scent change on skin?

Yes. The opening, heart, and base are designed to evolve over time, which is why the note pyramid is explained separately from the collections view.
