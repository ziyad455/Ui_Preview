The pyramid

Three layers. One silhouette.

The note pyramid is designed to be understood instantly and appreciated over time. Each tier has its own texture, but the full composition stays balanced and elegant.

Top notes

Immediate brightness.

Bergamot, grapefruit zest, pink pepper, and cardamom create the first lift. The opening is sharp, polished, and never sugary.

Bergamot Citrus Pink Pepper

Heart notes

The core personality.

Saffron, rose, suede, cedar, and incense define the middle of the fragrance. This is where warmth, structure, and depth begin to settle.

Saffron Rose Suede

Base notes

The long trail.

Oud, amber, tonka, musk, and vetiver hold the finish. The dry-down is dense, smooth, and designed to remain readable into the late evening.

Oud Amber Musk

Skin behavior

Notes move differently in daylight, heat, and air conditioning.

Dry skin tends to sharpen the opening, warmer skin expands the heart, and cooler evening air gives the base more room to linger. That is why every scent is composed with a clear opening, a defined core, and a deliberate finish.
