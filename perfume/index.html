Frequently asked

Short answers for the most common perfume questions.

Delivery

How long does delivery usually take?

Local orders are typically fulfilled in 2 to 3 business days. International timelines vary by destination and customs handling.

Payment

What payment methods are supported?

Use the consultation flow to confirm the preferred payment method. For the demo site, the primary path is a direct order request.

Sizes

What bottle sizes are available?

The house presents 100ml as the main format, with 50ml extrait-style editions available for special releases and travel.

Returns

Can I return unopened items?

Yes. Unopened items can be returned within 14 days of delivery. The return policy is designed to stay premium but clear.
